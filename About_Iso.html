About this site

I am a mathematician by education and inclination, a retired information systems developer by profession, and former harpsichordist by passion.

In the mathematical realm, over the years I have conducted independent research into polyhedra, primarily concerning stellations and partial regularity. This site is devoted to the little-understood edge-transitive (isotoxal) polyhedra.
