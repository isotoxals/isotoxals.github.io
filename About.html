About this site

I am a mathematician by education and inclination, a retired information systems developer by profession, and former harpsichordist by passion.

In the mathematical realm, over the years I have conducted independent research into polyhedra, primarily concerning stellations and partial regularity. The work on stellations is not yet ready for public consumption but I can offer this teaser: if one considers polyhedra properly as surfaces there are between 18 and 14,609 stellations of the regular Icosahedron. It all depends on what characteristics one allows in the surface structure of a face. In the meantime what I have to offer here are my results on partially-regular polyhedra.
